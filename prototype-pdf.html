Core Pain Points We Solve

Interview Anxiety

Students ace written exams but freeze in interviews. There's no safe, realistic space to practice — especially for Tier 2/3 college students who lack any exposure to corporate interview environments.

Resume Black Hole

75% of resumes are auto-rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human ever sees them. Most students don't even know ATS exists, let alone how to optimize their resumes for it.

Blind Career Choices

No data-driven guidance available. Students pick careers based on parental pressure or peer influence — not market demand, industry growth trends, or their own strengths and aptitudes.

Placement Infrastructure Gap

Top colleges have dedicated placement cells and direct employer connections. Tier 2/3 colleges have a notice board. The infrastructure gap is structural, systemic, and massive.
